Rochester, NY

A hub of research in Upstate, NY featuring universities such as the University of Rochester and the Rochester Institute of Technology. Also Rochester boasts leading companies like Bausch and Lomb, Harris Radio Communications and Kodak.

Biomedical Engineering

Biomedical Engineering combines the best of Biology and Engineering to solve comples medical problems. BMEs, as we're known, handle Mechanics, Electronics, Chemical Engineering and Computer Science on a daily basis to accomplish their goals.

In Brief

Started in 2016 by undergraduates from the University of Rochester, Androgen Research was incorporated to aid in the commercialization of the research projects from the Center for Medical Technology and Innovation (CMTI) program. The CMTI, a graduate Masters Program, gives students the opportunity to learn about designing medical devices and advancing the next generation of medical technology.

So, what do we do?

Androgen Research produces solutions in the Biologics and Surgical industries, including implants.
